Computer Science Mentors

Assisting, teaching, mentoring services for introductory computer science courses

What is CSM

CSM is a student-run organization that fascilitates the fluidity of CS61A and CS61B by providing group and one-on-one tutoring sessions.

Who we are

We are a group of UC Berkeley students passionate about teaching and helping classmates succeed in computer science.

Get Involved

Are you interested in gaining experience teaching and mentoring computer science students? Are you a student searching for additional tutoring for CS61A or CS61B? Would you like to partner with us?
